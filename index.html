Rules:

PPS 2077 will count up to your # from 1, with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping".
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong".
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong".
  4. Numbers divisible by 11 become "Match!"
  5. Numbers divisible by 50 become "Champion".
  6. Numbers divisible by 100 become "Legendary".
